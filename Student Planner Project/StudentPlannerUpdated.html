Your Data

This section provides information regarding how your data is stored and processed:

The UK GDPR requires us to inform our users what data is collected, and how and why it is processed. Users also have the right to request deletion of their data at any time. If you wish to delete your data from the application, scroll down to the bottom of this tab and click the button "Clear Local Storage".

Please note that there is no reversal to this process. Once you have deleted your data, you would have to re-enter all your tasks and modules.

What data is collected?

  • Task names and details
  • Module names and details
  • Assignments and exams schedules

How and where is the data stored?

The data is stored in the local storage of your browser. This means that the data is only accessible from the device and browser you used to enter it. The data is not sent to any server or third-party service, ensuring your privacy and security.

Because the data is stored locally, we recommend that you avoid using this application on public or shared devices. If you do, please remember to clear your local storage after use. We also recommend that you do not use this application on a private browsing session or in incognito mode, as the data may not be saved.

How is the data processed?

The data is processed locally in your browser using JavaScript. This means that the data is not sent to any server or third-party service, ensuring your privacy and security. The data is used to display your tasks and modules, and to allow you to add, edit, and delete them. The data is also used to display your assignments and exams schedules, and to allow you to add, edit, and delete them.

The data is not used for any other purpose, and is not shared with any third-party service. We do not collect any personal information from you, and we do not track your usage of the application. The data is only used to provide you with the functionality of the application, and to improve your experience.
